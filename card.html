Cheetah

The Cheetah has excellent eyesight; allowing it to see prey up to 5km away.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Top Speed: 112km/h
  • Average Lifespan: 17 years
  • Habitat: sub-Saharan Africa

Being the fastest mammal on Earth, the Cheetah has built up quite a reputation for itself. They can grow up to 1.4m long and are recognizable by their 'tear stripes' that start from their eyes down their nose.
